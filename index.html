Bio

They found each other through Craiglist. They were strangers before. Now they are ex-strangers.

The Extrangers are an indie alternative rock band with catchy, lovesick phrases and rad guitars.

They rehearse in a small bedroom near Lake Elsinor. They are strong advocates in the local music community. Most importantly, they throw one of the most entertaining band performances.

The Extrangers have released 1 album, 1 EP, and 2 singles. Keep an ear out for new music soon!

Listen to their music on their bandcamp.
